Behind the Scenes

With years of experience assisting executives and organizations, she ensures every project is actionable and impactful.

Her professional journey spans roles at Evolve8, Powur PBC, DataForce, NorthStar Exercise Science, Key Autism Center, Doordash, and Walmart. This diverse background enables The Black Copy to deliver innovative, precise, and efficient solutions tailored to each client.
